SECTOR 01 — DOSSIER

About

Industry Park Post formed in the gap between two demolitions — the kind of in-between space where nobody's watching and the acoustics are accidental but perfect. The sound sits somewhere between post-punk's nervous energy and the slow, structural weight of doom: tight rhythms under heavy air.

Every release is recorded on location — warehouses, car parks, the shells of buildings waiting to become something else. The room is part of the band.

Formed

2021, Freiburg
